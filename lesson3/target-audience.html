Target Audience

Description of audience:

The targeted audience is focused on people looking for opportunities to connect either for business growth opportunities, networking or gathering of resources for different purposes.
The general demographics of the targeted audience are the following:

  • Age: The mayority will be from 30 to 50 years.
  • Income: Middle to upper income brackets would be the mayority of the targeted audience.
  • Education: The vast mayority will have higher education
  • Family status: The broad audience has an established family

Persona 1:

Persona: Local Business Owner

  • Name: John Doe
  • Job: CEO of Luigi's Italian Cuisine
  • Demographics:
    • 55 years old
    • Married
    • Father of 3 children
    • Grandfather of 4 children
    • Have been working in the culinary industry for over 30 years
  • Goals and tasks:
    • After working and saving for his entire life he saved enough to open his own restaurant. After the first boisterous years of his business he is strugling to get more attention and is looking for new opportunities to promote his business.
    • He also needs to improve his business skills on networking
  • Environment: He knows how to use a computer but is not a tech savvy. His computer skills are basic and is comfortable using it for simple tasks. Spends the mayority of his time working at the restaurant so he just does some research for his business for short periods of time during the week.

Persona 2:

Persona: Entrepreneur

  • Name: Jessica Smith
  • Job: Research for own business
  • Demographics:
    • 24 years old
    • Common-law partner
    • No children
    • Recently graduated from University of Winnipeg with a Bachelors of Arts in Contemporary Dance
  • Goals and tasks:
    • After graduating School she is looking for opportunities to open up a business for a dance studio in the city.
    • She is unaware of the business requirements for opening up a business in her industry and would like to know more.
    • She needs resources (both financial and mentorship) for her business idea
  • Environment: She is determined to succeed in her goals. She spends several hours per day gathering all the resources she can, both online and in-person. She is aware there might be competition in the city so she is perfecting her elevator pitch for her business idea.

Scenarios:

Scenario 1

John is a business who is struggling to attract new customers to his restaurant. While searching online he found the WCC website looking for local event and marketing oppotunities for his business. Finding the event he is looking for will be the perfect oppotunity for him to bring his networking to the next level.

Scenario 2

Jessica is doing research to open up his own dance studio. She is unware of the local regulations and permits required to open her proposed business. She finds the WCC website looking for resources that can help her start her business.

Scenario 3

Andrew is a local city councelor looking to implement new regulations on the retail sector that could impact drastically in the local businesses. He reaches out to the Winnipeg Chamber of Commerce in look of feedback and events where he can propose his ideas and gain momentum for his campaign.

Scenario 4

Peter recently graduated from high school and is looking for a job. He reaches out to the WCC to look for business that are currently hiring.

Scenario 5

Maria would love to work in the financial sector. She studied a long time ago but doesn't have experience. She is looking for a volunteer position where she can demonstrate her potential and gain skills. She reaches out to the WCC in look for volunteering opportunities at the local financial businesses.
