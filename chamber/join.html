Membership Fees and Descriptions

Non Profit Member:

This is only for non-profit organization, covers all benefits from the Bronze Membership

Bronze Member:

Price: $350
Includes monthly training, 20% on all chamber events and advertising on the directory

Silver Member:

Price: $450
Includes all bronze benefits plus an additional 20% discount on the events and one business workship per semester completely free!

Gold Member:

Price: $565
Includes all Bronze and Silver benefits, 80% discount on chamber events and free spotlight advertising on the chamber website!
